Who we are

Herban Health
at a glance.

Herban Health was started by a group of East Palo Alto residents to help people with limited resources help
themselves to live in a more balanced and healthy way.

We believe that when members of a community are involved in the decisions about how healthcare is
provided that the results are improved and more sustainable.

Herban Healthâ€™s mission is to listen to the health care needs of the community and then provide free holistic health care services to meet these needs.

COME TO A FREE CLINIC OR CLASS

Herban Health offers a regular schedule of acupuncture clinics and meditation groups. We also hold a variety of holistic health classes on topics including diabetes, digestive problems, stress reduction, insomnia, children's health, exercise, stopping smoking, losing weight, and high blood pressure.

Acupuncture has been proved through controlled trials to be an effective treatment for many conditions, including headache, depression, nausea and vomiting, hypertension, and various types of pain such as low back pain, The World Health Organization has a more complete list.(WHO review of acupuncture, section 3)

We offer our services free of charge although donations are welcome but optional. If you are interested in learning more, please see our Clinic and Class Calendar. Everyone is welcome!
