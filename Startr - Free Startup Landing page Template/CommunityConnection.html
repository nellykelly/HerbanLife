Education, Youth

The purpose of the Holistic Healthcare Community Outreach and Support Project. (HHCOSP) is:
1. to promote the access of holistic healthcare to communities with limited access to healthcare through creating and building on exisiting models.
2. to develop partnerships with community based organizations who are providing healthcare in these communities in order to link organizations and individuals who practice the many forms of holistic healthcare.
3. to provide technical assistance for community based organizations which have an interest in integrating holistic healthcare into their services.
4. to develop ways to increase the number of people of color and medically underserved people entering the holistic healthcare field.

One of the ways that we are furthering this purpose is to create a Bay Area Healthcare Consortium, where community based organizations that are serving medically underserved people can be linked to holistic healthcare providers willing to donate their services. We are organizing a pilot project that will elaborate on our model of working within community based organizations. We will initially take a designated number of clients in a community based organization, and offer a course of from 2 to ten weeks of holistic healthcare services to enhance the goal of the community based organization as well as further Herban Healthâ€™s goal of providing holistic healthcare to those with limited resources in a manner that increases their own involvement in their healthcare. The goal of the pilot project is to:
1. Provide services where they are needed.
2. Develop systems in order to facilitate the matching of needs with resources.
3. Document and critique the results of this project in order to expand its use.
